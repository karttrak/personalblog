Google Analytics

Having analytics for a website is critical for helping creators figure out what is working and what isn't. You can try to repeat what people love, and you know to avoid what disinterests people. I don't really want analytics for this reason right now. I have so few readers — or, at least, I assume I have few readers; it's hard to tell without analytics — that there is not much information I can glean from tracking visits. But why I do want to add Google Analytics sooner rather than later is because I want to add analytics to all my pages. Going longer without analytics just means more and more posts that I have to circle back to and add the analytics tag.

It will be nice to have a way to see how much traffic my posts are getting, even if it's just "Hey! Here's Don right after I told him to go check out my latest post!" It will also be good experience for future web development projects. So before I get too deep into any other R&D projects, I need to learn about Google Analytics.
