Changing the site name again

This has been a busy month for naming this blog. We started the month as Kyle of Write, which had been the name of the blog from day 1. However, that was just a bad pun, and I don't really want my own name to be contained in the blog name. It also didn't really have much to do with my intention for the blog, other than trying to write every day. After a stint of infrequent posts, I sat down and thought about why I wanted to create this blog. I want to work on my skills as a web developer. I also want to get over needing everything to be perfect all the time. Lastly, I want to show that you don't need everything figured out before starting a project. Just start, and you will get better from there. As a result, Incremental Improvements was born.
